Matchit Game

Rules for the Tile Matching Game

Objective The goal of the game is to match all pairs of tiles on the grid.

Game Grid You will be presented with a grid of face-down tiles. These tiles are randomly shuffled at the beginning of the game.

Matching Pairs Tiles have matching pairs. To win, you must find and match all pairs. Matching pairs will be removed from the grid.

Memory The game relies on your memory. Try to remember the positions and patterns on the tiles as you play to make successful matches.

Time Limit Some variations of the game may have a time limit. Complete the matching within the specified time to win.

Scoring Your score may be based on factors like the number of moves or time taken to complete the game. Strive for a high score!
